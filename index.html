OUR PROCESS

Every vehicle receives our signature 7-step detailing process, perfected over thousands of vehicles.

1. INITIAL ASSESSMENT

We perform a 50-point inspection using digital paint meters and specialized lighting to document your vehicle's exact condition.

Paint Thickness Mapping

Using advanced digital gauges, we measure paint thickness at 20+ points to identify previous repairs and thin areas.

2. DEEP CLEANING

Our pH-balanced snow foam and two-bucket wash system safely removes contaminants without scratching your paint.

Decontamination

Clay bar treatment and iron fallout remover eliminate embedded contaminants that washing alone can't remove.

3. PAINT CORRECTION

Using precision polishing machines and proprietary compound systems, we remove swirls and restore gloss.

Multi-Stage Polishing

Our 3-step process (compound, polish, refine) achieves mirror-like finishes on even the softest paints.

4. PROTECTION

We apply ceramic coatings or sealants that chemically bond to your paint for long-lasting protection.

Nano-Ceramic Technology

Our 9H hardness coatings provide 3-5 years of protection against UV, chemicals, and environmental damage.

5. INTERIOR DETAILING

Every surface is cleaned, conditioned, and protected using techniques that preserve your interior materials.

Steam Cleaning

Our 300Â°F steam sanitizes without harsh chemicals, removing stains and odors from even delicate materials.

6. FINAL INSPECTION

Under specialized lighting, we perform a meticulous quality check to ensure perfection in every detail.

Quality Assurance

Our 50-point checklist ensures every inch of your vehicle meets our exacting standards before delivery.

7. DELIVERY

Your vehicle is returned in a climate-controlled environment with a full walkthrough of the work performed.

Aftercare Consultation

We provide detailed maintenance instructions and complimentary checkups to keep your vehicle looking its best.
